About Us

Consumers Downtown Association (CDA) is Kenya’s independent, self-funded, non-political and non-profit Association committed to consumer protection, education, research, consultancy, anti-counterfeits campaign, and consumerism and customer-care issues. 

The Association is registered in Kenya under societies Act cap.108. Consumers Downtown Association works towards creating a fair, just and safe market for all players in sectors of Kenya’s economy and improving quality of consumer goods and services.

We draw our mandate from Article 46 of the Constitution of Kenya 2010, the Consumer Protection Act, 2012 and the Competition Act, Cap 504 among other legislations. The legal framework provides background and establishment of consumer interventions and high thresholds for consumer protection in both private and public sectors of Kenya’s economy.

Our vission

To be a reliable, caring and understanding consumer protection organization of choice across Kenya and the Region.

Our mission

To educate, inform & inspire consumers thereby restoring their confidence and trust in the market.

Values

  • Integrity
  • Transparency & accountability
  • Good Governance
  • Justice and fairness
  • Honesty
  • Team Work
  • Inclusivity

Our Mandate

The organization’s mandate is to protect, promote, develop, pursue consumer interests and rights as entrenched in Article 46 of the Constitution of Kenya 2010, the Consumer Protection Act, 2012 and the Competition Act, Cap 504 to create an enabling environment for all businesses to competitively explore the market.
