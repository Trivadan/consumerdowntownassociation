Fredrick Kayiya

(Founder)

Fred is the Founding member of CFCD . Prior to the initiation of CFCD, He has spent several years working as a social researcher for different entities.He is specialized in slum set- up and has worked in different Organizations, Including; Kosmos solution International and Mapkibera, serving at different capacities.

His interest in social sciences is hooked to his undergraduate at the University of Nairobi where he is pursued his Bachelors Degree in Project Planning and Management. Prior to that, he had studied business management at Kenya Methodist University.

Diana Mueni

(Co.Founder)

She is a community worker in Kibera Slums from 2011-2013.She has worked as a citizen reporter for map Kibera and also been volunteering at Angles of Hope Kibera , as a Social Worker.

She is currently a 4th year student of University of Nairobi pursuing Bachelor of Commerce, holds a Diploma in Human Resource management from the same University .
