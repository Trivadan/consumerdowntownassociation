Membership

How to Become a Member

Become a member at any of the below categories by filling in and submitting a complete membership application form; Take advantage of the low membership rates and join now. Please note that membership is subject to vetting. This would help avoid potential cases of conflict of interest.

Categories of membership;

Consumer Downtown Association (CDA) is a membership organization with members across the country. The following are the membership categories with respective subscriptions:

  • Individual Membership - Kshs.2,000 plus one off  Ksh.500 joining fee
  • SMEs/Corporate Member - Kshs.20,000 plus one off  Kshs.10,000
  • Corporate Benefactor Member - Kshs.100,000

Benefits

  • Free Arbitration for your case.
  • Free Representation
  • Consultancy at discounted rates.
  • Networking opportunities at CDA forums/events
  • Free access to information & Education

About CDA

The Consumers Downtown Association (CDA) is Kenya’s independent, self-funded, non-political and non-profit Association committed to consumer protection, education, research, consultancy, anti-counterfeits campaign, and consumerism and customer-care issues.
